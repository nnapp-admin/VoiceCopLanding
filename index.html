Frequently Asked Questions.

What is VoiceCopilot AI?

VoiceCopilot AI is an always-on AI voice agent that answers inbound calls instantly, understands customer intent, and takes real actions like booking appointments, routing calls, and sending follow-ups. Outbound calling is also available.

Is VoiceCopilot AI built for inbound or outbound calls?

VoiceCopilot AI is inbound-first by design. It ensures zero missed calls and handles conversations in real time. Outbound calling (reminders, follow-ups, callbacks) is supported via Outbound Shuttle (New).

What does the one-time $500 setup fee include?

The setup fee covers: AI voice agent creation Voice configuration (499+ voices) Knowledge and intent setup Call workflows and routing Phone number integration (Twilio or SIP) Testing and onboarding support This is a one-time cost, not recurring.

How does pricing work after setup?

VoiceCopilot AI uses usage-based pricing. 1 credit = 1 minute of AI voice conversation You choose a monthly credit plan Additional credits can be purchased at $0.35 per minute

Does VoiceCopilot AI replace human agents?

VoiceCopilot AI handles most routine inbound calls. It escalates to human agents when needed, with full context, improving efficiency without removing human oversight.

What industries can use VoiceCopilot AI?

VoiceCopilot AI is industry-agnostic and widely used across: Healthcare, hospitality, salons, HVAC, real estate, legal, education, retail, construction, finance, and more.
